About Us




Who are we, in a nutshell?

Our website is dedicated to teaching people about the uses and dangers of not having contraceptives. Contraceptives protect against more than unwanted pregnancies, they also protect against STI’s. STIs have no age limits and if you are sexually active. Which are just as dangerous as unwanted pregnancies, they come in many different forms and can be minimized by usage of contraceptives. That seems like a no brainer for most people, if you don’t want an STI, then use some form of contraceptive but most people cannot do that.
